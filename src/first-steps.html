FIRST BUSINESS-STEPS

The first business that Oskar became the founder of was a sports nutrition online-store in Germany. In 2010 the entrepreneur created Sapato, a company specializing in the online sales of new shoe collections. Then the first live-TV store in Russia was created — called Shopping Live, which quickly became the leader in sales among its competitors.

In 2012 Sapato was acquired by Ozon, and Oskar became a co-shareholder of Ozon.ru. At the same time Hartmann sold the main share of Shopping Live which allowed him to obtain capital to launch a number of his own enterprises: Teamo, Carprice, Relevant Media, HomeMarket, Aktivo, Vitaportal, AutoSpot, Iqcard, Hlebnoe mesto and several Internet companies.
