OUTREACH AND COMMUNITIES

Oskar Hartmann is one of the most open and highly-demanded speakers in Russia. An entrepreneur personally demonstrates an active approach to business and life, as well as the benefits of positive thinking. Oskar devotes a significant part of his time to the development of entrepreneurship around the world. His initiatives are aimed at supporting and unveiling the potential of young talented people and successful entrepreneurs. More than twenty thousand people a year take part in programs and seminars created by Oskar.


Oskar not only creates, but also oversees many communities:

  1. The Preaktum Leadership Program is an educational ecosystem that connects companies and young entrepreneurs. Interactive courses from leading experts of Russian and global companies are held on our own educational platform. Participants study marketing, sales, programming, business models and economics.
  2. Equium is a business community that unites the most influential entrepreneurs — people who are not only able to build a multibillion-dollar company, but also support socially transformative activities that change the life of society for the better. As its main goal, this community sees a qualitative change in macroeconomic processes and the improvement of human well-being through the development of entrepreneurship and the popularization of positive role models.
  3. In addition, Oskar Hartmann holds the position of Chairman of the Board of Trustees of the Rybakov Foundation, a non-profit organization created to support Russian entrepreneurship, as well as social non-profit projects and education.
