INVESTMENTS

Oskar Harmann is a venture capitalist and business angel. He is a co-founder and partner of Fastline Ventures, Simile Ventures Partners, and LARIX funds. Hartmann has invested in many international companies: Auto1 Group, Casacanda (fab.com), Frontier Car Group, FashionAndYou, Sukar, Nanjacart, June Homes, Flaschenpost.de. Oskar’s venture investment portfolio (including the Hartmann family holding Hartmann Holdings) includes more than seventy companies. Five of them have already reached a capitalization of more than $ 1 billion.
