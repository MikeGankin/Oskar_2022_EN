BIOGRAPHY

Oskar was born into a family of Russian Germans in Kazakhstan on May 14, 1982. When Oskar turned eight years old, his parents decided to move to Germany, where the future entrepreneur spent all his youth. At the age of seventeen, Hartmann flew to the United States where he completed exchange studies at Tuscarawas Valley High School in order to master English in which he eventually succeeded. After coming back to Germany he graduated with honors from the technical gymnasium. When it came to serving in the army, Oskar chose an alternative civilian service, with which he proceeded in Russia during the whole 2004 at the Children’s Clinical Hospital.
