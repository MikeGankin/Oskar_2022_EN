„You are what you do„

The life strategy and business plan of entrepreneur Oskar Hartmann is to achieve everything through active actions, be faster than others and not be afraid to try new things all the time. From the age of 11, when young Hartmann got his first job, until today, when his companies have reached a capitalization of five billion US dollars, Oscar was guided by the philosophy of action.
