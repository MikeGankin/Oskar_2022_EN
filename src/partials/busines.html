You are what you do.

The life strategy and business plan of entrepreneur Oskar Hartmann is to achieve everything through active actions, be faster than others and not be afraid to try new things all the time. From the age of 11, when young Hartmann got his first job, until today, when his companies have reached a capitalization of five billion US dollars, Oskar was guided by the philosophy of action. An entrepreneur, according to Oskar Hartmann, is someone who can take an illusory idea and put it into practice.

Oskar has founded and is developing many companies: Aktivo — Joint investment in real estate — low-risk rental business. CarPrice is a modern service for fast car repurchase. FactoryMarket is an international innovative project that combines practices from e-commerce and offline trading. It works according to the concept of  integrated omnichannel sales.

Home Market is a chain of convenience stores at low prices. Currently there are 119 network divisions operating in Russia, mainly in Moscow and Moscow Oblast. Klyutch is a network of offices and coworking spaces. Ready-made solutions for businesses of any scale. Teamo is an online dating service for serious relationships. In «Teamo.ru» implemented a unique selection of psychologically compatible partners. Each user of  the site undergoes psychological testing.

Relevant Media is an innovative online media company, a leader in the segment of professional content for users looking for the answers to simple questions online. Relevant Media helps readers solve everyday tasks, saves time and money, and answers hundreds of thousands of practical questions. HomeMarket is a chain of convenience stores at low prices. Vitaportal is a portal about health and medicine. In addition to news and articles, various services are hosted on the site: online diet, BMI calculator and others. All materials and articles presented on the portal are checked by specialists, doctors and experts.
