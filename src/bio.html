BIOGRAPHY

Oskar was born into a family of Russian Germans in Kazakhstan on May 14, 1982. When Oskar turned eight years old, his parents decided to move to Germany, where the future entrepreneur spent all his youth. At the age of seventeen, Hartmann flew to the United States where he completed exchange studies at Tuscarawas Valley High School in order to master English in which he eventually succeeded. After coming back to Germany he graduated with honors from the technical gymnasium.

When it came to serving in the army, Oskar chose an alternative civilian service, with which he proceeded in Russia during the whole 2004 at the Children’s Clinical Hospital. While in Russia, he took the opportunity to learn Russian language. Afterwards Oskar Hartmann entered WHU University in Koblenz and graduated with a degree in International Economics. During his studies, Oskar, being one of the best students, interned at multinational companies: BMW in Malaysia, GameGoods in Cologne, as well as at Boston Consulting Group. He spent several semesters as an exchange MBA student at the University of Hawaii (USA) and at the Financial Academy (Russia).
